About Us

the highschool board

is a mission-driven not-for-profit organization that connects students to college success and opportunity. Founded in 1900 was created to expand access to higher education. Today, the membership association is made up of over 6,000 of the world s leading educational institutions and is dedicated to promoting excellence and equity in education.

Benefits of Membership

As a College Board member, your organization is part of a community of educators that has been working together for more than 100 years to deliver opportunity to students. Our members from more than 6,000 institutions and organizations worldwide provide invaluable insight and drive the mission of the College Board Learn more about the benefits of membership.

Become a Member

When an organization becomes a member, every professional in that organization has the opportunity to take advantage of all College Board membership has to offer. Learn more. Complete an application.
